# Online Sales Analysis – 2018  
(SQL-Based Business Insights & Recommendations)

---

## Sales & Profit Performance by Category

### Key Insights
- Electronics generated the highest total sales, indicating strong customer demand.
- Clothing delivered the highest profit, reflecting better margins.
- Furniture showed moderate sales and profit, indicating optimization opportunities.
- High sales volume does not always translate into high profitability.

### Business Recommendations
- Optimize pricing and supplier costs in Electronics to improve margins.
- Expand and promote Clothing products due to strong profitability.
- Review logistics, sourcing, and discount strategies for Furniture.

---

## Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

### Key Insights
- Sales show clear seasonal fluctuations.
- Certain months consistently outperform others.
- Low-performing months indicate reduced customer activity.

### Business Recommendations
- Plan inventory and staffing for peak months.
- Introduce promotions during low-sales periods.
- Align marketing spend with seasonal demand.

---

## Product Performance – Sub-Category Analysis

### Key Insights
- A small number of sub-categories drive most sales.
- Some sub-categories show high sales but low or negative profit.
- Profitability varies significantly within the same category.

### Business Recommendations
- Promote high-profit sub-categories aggressively.
- Re-evaluate pricing and supplier contracts for loss-making products.
- Consider discontinuing consistently underperforming sub-categories.

---

## Regional Sales Analysis (City & State Level)

### Key Insights
- Sales are concentrated in a few top cities and states.
- These regions act as primary revenue drivers.
- Several regions show untapped potential.

### Business Recommendations
- Prioritize marketing and faster delivery in top regions.
- Use high-performing regions for pilot launches.
- Investigate pricing, awareness, and logistics gaps in low-performing regions.

---

## Customer Value Analysis – Loyalty Program

### Key Insights
- A small group of customers contributes a significant share of revenue.
- High-value customers demonstrate repeat purchase behavior.
- Retention of these customers is critical.

### Business Recommendations
- Enroll top customers in loyalty or VIP programs.
- Offer exclusive benefits and early access.
- Focus retention strategies on high-value customers.

---

## Payment Mode Usage Analysis

### Key Insights
- Cash on Delivery (COD) is the most frequently used payment mode.
- Digital payments show strong adoption but trail COD.
- Heavy COD usage increases operational risk.

### Business Recommendations
- Improve COD operations to reduce failed deliveries.
- Encourage digital payments through discounts or cashback.
- Gradually reduce dependency on COD.

---

## Overall Business Impact
- Identified key revenue and profit drivers.
- Highlighted seasonal demand patterns.
- Enabled targeted customer retention strategies.
- Improved understanding of payment behavior.

---
